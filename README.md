### Advanced Filament Estimation Utility

**Ungoliant** is a high-precision desktop utility designed for 3D printing professionals and enthusiasts. It provides real-time estimation of remaining filament based on weight or visual percentage, helping you avoid failed prints due to running out of material.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

- **SPOOL ANALYSIS**: Dual-mode input for precision weighing or manual percentage estimation.
- **REAL-TIME VISUALIZER**: Dynamic spool display that drains as you input print requirements.
- **COST TRACKING**: Live calculation of print costs based on material weight.
- **MATERIAL LIBRARY**: Optimized presets for PLA, PETG, ABS, and more.
- **LOW FILAMENT WARNING**: Visual alerts when your spool cannot sustain the required print.

---

## üìñ Usage Instructions

1.  **Select Material**: Choose your filament type to set the correct density.
2.  **Analyze Spool**: Enter your "Gross Weight" (Spool + Filament) or toggle **Estimate by %** if you don't have a scale handy.
3.  **Input Print Size**: Paste the required weight for your print from your slicer.
4.  **Review Results**: Check the "Remaining After Print" and "Estimated Cost".
5.  **Visualize**: Watch the spool graphic to see the predicted fill level.
